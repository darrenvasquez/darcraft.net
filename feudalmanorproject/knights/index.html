The Knights of the Manor

Knights were the defenders of the manor. They were specificially trained to fight in case of an emergency, whether it be inside the manor or for the king. They fought with swords and other hand to hand combat, rode on horseback, and usually wore metal armor. While their main job was to fight and protect, some of them were given land in exchange for protection (called vassals)
