The Lord of the Manor

The Lords maintained land, used for a manor, given to them by the king. They were very wealthy, and were in charge of keeping the land well. They were expected by the king to provide an army of soldiers whenever needed. They were the "boss" of the manor. He would be in charge of providing his king with taxes, such as crops, cattle, and more. This was usually done by the Serfs and Knights working for the Lord.
