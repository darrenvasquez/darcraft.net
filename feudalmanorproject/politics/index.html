The Politics of the Manor

Manors were ruled by Lords and Nobles, watching over and maintaining the land that their king gave them. The Lords and Nobles dealt with most issues that rose in their manor.
Knights were allowed to own land, but were lower in the social class. Their main purpose was to provide protection to the Manor in case of invasion or other catastrophic event.
Serfs, also known as peasants, were at the bottom of the social class (little to no power), and were limited to working for their lords in exchange for food, a house, and protection.
