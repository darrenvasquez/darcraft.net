DarCraft Mobile Alerts

Sign up for DarCraft mobile alerts to receive push notifications on your iPhone or Android device when someone files a new in-game report

Step 1: Download AirGram from the Apple App Store or the Google Play Store

Step 2: Sign up with your email address and create a password

Step 3: Log into the server on your computer and type /reportnotify [email] with the email you created your AirGram account with to sign up for mobile alerts
